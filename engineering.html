Engineering

E in STEM stands for Engineering. It refers to the application of scientific knowledge to design, build, and create new products, structures, machines, and systems. Engineering involves using critical thinking, problem solving, and design skills to develop solutions to practical problems that have a real-world impact. Some examples of engineering fields include mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, civil engineering, and biomedical engineering.
